Mental Illness is a common condition that many students face, whether it may be negligble for some individuals, for others it can hinder their grades, create stress, and even take their life.

Life doesn't have straight lines, i obstacles get thrown our way and when we conquer then we grow as individuals. However sometimes one will face a problem where it seems like there is no light at the end of the tunnel, that their world is dark, and cold. This page is designed with those individuals in mind.
Mindcheck is an organization that started up with the support of the Vancouver Canucks after one of their players Rick Rypien committed suicide, they help individuals asses mental illness, and help cure it. From drug addictions, to anxiety these people are at your side every step of the way.
